# Airband Monitoring - First Successful Reception

Date: 2026-06-12

Location:
Gdańsk, Poland

Equipment:

* RTL-SDR Blog V4
* SDR++
* Dipole antenna

Frequency:
127.275 MHz

Mode:
AM

Bandwidth:
10 kHz

Observations:
Initially the voice signal was very weak. After increasing the AM bandwidth from 1 kHz to 10 kHz, the audio became clear and intelligible.

A male voice was received on the frequency. During reception, an aircraft was visually observed flying overhead, suggesting the transmission was related to local air traffic operations.

Lessons Learned:

* Airband communications use AM.
* Bandwidth settings have a major impact on voice intelligibility.
* Correlating RF signals with real-world events helps validate reception.
* Aircraft communications are intermittent and require patience to monitor.
